Vision

To make business self-driving and achieve the expansion of economic and industrial infrastructure.

We integrate multimodal AI for multi-faceted demand understanding with file- and code-base system and integration. By structuring this end-to-end process, we realize "autonomous business operations" that continuously provide value without human intervention.

Mission

  • Plan, build, and operate businesses that meet market demand.

    From multimodal market analysis, we provide valuable services that address those needs.

  • Building Project "CRAai".

    Corporate tasks are executed through file processing, and the results are accumulated as files. We integrate various service APIs using MCP and operate the business accordingly.

  • Build infrastructure for interoperability among AI agents.

    We enable AI agents to coordinate as needed for business and provide next-generation societal infrastructure.

Services

  • Business

    Business portfolio aligned with market demand

    Through businesses such as those in our projects, we respond to societal demand. From demand research to the creation of new businesses.

  • CRAai

    Autonomous system (Project CRAai)

    Building a system that operates business autonomously through the use of various service APIs and MCP that integrates them.

  • MCP

    AI Agent infrastructure

    Trading of AI agents themselves; provision of systems that enable AI agents to operate seamlessly.

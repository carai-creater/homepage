Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

CRAai (“we,” “us,” or “our”) recognizes the importance of protecting personal information and handles it in accordance with applicable laws and this Privacy Policy (“Policy”).

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information in connection with our services:

  • When you contact us: Name, email address, and inquiry content
  • When you apply for a position: Name, contact details, work history, and other information provided in your application
  • Access information: IP address, browser type, access date and time, referrer URL, and similar data for understanding how our website is used

3. Use of Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries
  • Conducting recruitment and communicating with applicants
  • Improving our services and developing new offerings
  • Compliance with applicable laws

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose personal information to third parties without consent, except where required by law, where necessary to protect life, body, or property and obtaining consent is difficult, or in other legally permitted circumstances.

5. Security

We take appropriate measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of personal information and review such measures as necessary.

6. Contact

For inquiries regarding this Policy or requests for access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, please contact:

info@craai.co.jp

7. Changes

We may revise this Policy. When we do, we will post the updated Policy on our website and, for material changes, notify you as appropriate.
